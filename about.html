The current version of this App depends on receiving train and station data from BART's API. At the time of writing this, the API returns data for trains occuring within the next hour. Therefore, this service does not help to choose a departure time more than an hour in advance.

BART's operating hours are:

  • Weekdays (4:00 am - Midnight)
  • Saturday (6:00 am - Midnight)
  • Sunday (8:00 am - Midnight)
On weekdays trains run every 15 minutes.
